FAQ

Do you have vegetable broth?
No, we do not. Except the hot and sour vegetable soup, we can make it for vegetarian for you!
Do you take reservations?
We take reservations only for groups of five and up, exclusions may apply. Call us at blank.
Do you do delivery/takeout?
We do delivery using third-party apps including Grubhub, Doordash, and Ubereats. We only take pickup orders off the phone!
What are your vegetarian options?
We have vegetable pho (chicken broth), fried tofu summer rolls, fried tofu vermicelli, stir fry vegetables over rice, hot and sour vegetable soup, and several vegetable entrees.
Do you have outdoor seating?
Yes, we do!
What are your payment options?
We take credit cards, debit cards, Apple Pay, and cash!
What are your opening hours?
We are open everyday except Monday from 11:00 am to 8:30pm!
Do you have alcohol?
Yes, we do! We have bottled beers, wine, sake, soju, and hard selzters.
Can I bring my own bottle of wine?
Yes, you may, but we charge a 10 dollar cork fee!
